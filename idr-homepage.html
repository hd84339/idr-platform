Who We Are

Industry-Led.
Academically Grounded.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute purpose-built for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments. We exist to close the critical talent gap between academic theory and the operational reality of digital risk in sectors where failure is not an option.

Through our UK university partnership, IDR bridges academic insight with real-world project experience. Our graduates and practitioners are not just trained — they are deployed into live engagements, bringing fresh rigour and frameworks to organisations navigating an increasingly complex digital threat landscape, from AI governance to critical infrastructure resilience.

Our Model

Three Pillars.
One Mission.

IDR's integrated model connects education, innovation and advisory practice — creating a continuous pipeline of talent and knowledge that benefits practitioners, employers and the broader digital risk ecosystem.

01

Academy

Structured training programmes and intensive bootcamps for students and working professionals seeking to build or deepen digital and cyber risk expertise. From foundational modules to specialist upskilling.

Bootcamps Cohort Training Certifications CPD
02

Innovation & Incubation

Developing proprietary IP, next-generation risk models and AI governance frameworks. IDR's incubation function supports emerging tools and methodologies that address future digital risk challenges head-on.

IP Development AI Governance Risk Models R&D
03

Advisory Services

Expert guidance helping organisations design, implement and mature digital risk frameworks. From NIST and ISO 27001 implementation to NIS2 compliance, IDR's practitioners embed alongside internal teams.

NIST ISO 27001 NIS2 Assurance
